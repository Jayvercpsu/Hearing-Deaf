CommPass is an interactive Augmented and Alternative Communication (AAC) platform designed to bridge the gap between the Deaf and hearing communities by centering on Filipino Sign Language (FSL). Through its core features, the platform transforms communication into a shared responsibility rather than a one-sided effort. By making FSL more accessible and engaging, CommPass aims to prove the effectiveness of digital AAC tools in fostering true inclusivity and to inspire the continued development of technology that empowers all users to connect without barriers.
